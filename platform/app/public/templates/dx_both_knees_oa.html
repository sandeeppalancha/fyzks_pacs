DEPARTMENT OF RADIOLOGY

X-RAY BOTH KNEES AP & LATERAL STANDING VIEW


Decreased medial aspect of tibiofemoral joint spaces with evidence of
subchondral sclerosis and periarticular osteophytes.
Reduced patellofemoral joint spaces.
Very minimal suprapatellar effusion seen.

IMPRESSION: Features are suggestive of
Degenerative joint disease (Osteoarthritis) - Grade-3.

**Kellgren- Lawrence Radiographic Grading Scale of Osteoarthritis of the Tiobiofemoral joint.
Grade 0 - No radiographic findings of osteoarthritis
Grade 1 - Minute osteophytes of doubtful clinical significance.
Grade 2 - Definite Osteophytes with unimpaired joint space.
Grade 3 - Definite osteophytes with moderate joint spac narrowing
Grade 4 - Definite Osteophytes with severe joint space narrowing and subchondral sclerosis.
